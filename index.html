4-Day Training Plan (Smith Machine–Compatible)

Strength + endurance plan formatted for a simple Netlify deploy. Expand each day to view details.

Single-file site • Netlify-ready

Weekly Split

Rest days are flexible. A common flow is Mon Day 1, Tue Day 2, Thu Day 4, Fri Day 5.

Day 1
Lower (Hinge) + Intervals
Posterior chain strength + short hard conditioning
Day 2
Upper (Push/Pull) + Zone 2
Upper strength + aerobic base
Day 3
Rest / Walk / Mobility
Light movement, recovery, optional stretching
Day 4
Lower (Squat) + Conditioning
Leg strength + controlled circuit
Day 5
Upper (Pull) + Carries
Back/posture strength + loaded endurance

Tip: If your lower back feels worse after training (not just normal muscle fatigue), reduce load and keep reps smooth.

Endurance Included

You’ll do both Zone 2 (easy) and intervals (hard/brief).

  • Zone 2: 20–30 min at a pace where you can talk in full sentences.
  • Intervals: short bursts (30s hard / 90s easy) to build capacity without long grind.
  • Carries: farmer’s walks for core, grip, and real-world endurance.

DAY 1 — LOWER BODY (HINGE FOCUS) + SHORT CARDIO

Posterior chain + short intervals
Strength + Intervals

Strength

Smith Machine Romanian Deadlift

4×6–8

  • Stand slightly forward of the bar
  • Push hips back, soft knees
  • Feel hamstrings, not lower back

Dumbbell Bulgarian Split Squat

3×8 / leg

Longer stride = more glute/hip. Keep control.

Hip Thrust (Smith or Barbell)

3×10–12

Pause 1s at the top, ribs down, squeeze glutes.

Seated or Lying Hamstring Curl

3×12

Full range, smooth reps.

Dead Bugs

3×10 / side

Slow. Keep lower back gently pressed into the floor.

Conditioning (8–10 min)

Bike / Rower / Treadmill Intervals

30 sec hard • 90 sec easy × 5 rounds

Hard = breathing heavy, but maintain control. Easy = recover fully.

DAY 2 — UPPER BODY (PUSH + PULL) + ZONE 2

Upper strength + aerobic base
Strength + Zone 2

Strength

Incline Dumbbell Bench Press

4×6–8

Stop 1–2 reps before failure; keep shoulders packed.

Chest-Supported Machine or DB Row

4×8–10

Pull elbows back, squeeze upper back for 1s.

Seated Dumbbell Shoulder Press

3×8

Neutral grip if shoulders feel better.

Cable Lateral Raises

3×12–15

Control down; don’t swing.

Cable Triceps Pushdowns

3×12–15

Full extension; elbows stay pinned.

Endurance

Zone 2 Cardio

20–30 minutes

  • Brisk incline walk
  • Easy jog
  • Bike
  • You should be able to talk in sentences.

DAY 4 — LOWER BODY (SQUAT FOCUS) + METCON

Squat pattern + controlled circuit
Strength + Circuit

Strength

Smith Machine Squat

4×6–8

  • Feet slightly forward
  • Sit “down and back”
  • Neutral spine, brace hard

Goblet Squat (DB)

3×10

Reinforces patterning and upright torso.

Walking Lunges (DB)

3×10 / leg

Long stride, controlled knee tracking.

Leg Extension

3×12–15

Smooth reps, no bouncing.

Side Planks

3×30–45 sec / side

Hips stacked, ribs down, steady breathing.

Conditioning (10–12 min)

Circuit — 3–4 rounds

  • 15 kettlebell swings
  • 12 bodyweight squats
  • 30 sec rest
  • Keep breathing controlled.

DAY 5 — UPPER BODY (PULL) + LOADED ENDURANCE

Back/posture + carries
Pull + Carries

Strength

Lat Pulldown or Assisted Pull-Ups

4×8–10

Full stretch at top, pull to upper chest.

Single-Arm Dumbbell Row

3×10 / side

Brace with free hand; avoid twisting.

Face Pulls

3×15

Elbows high, squeeze rear delts/upper back.

Rear Delt Flyes (DB or Cable)

3×12–15

Control; don’t shrug.

EZ-Bar or DB Curls

3×10–12

Full ROM, steady reps.

Endurance Finisher

Farmer’s Carries

4–6 rounds • 30–45 sec carry • 60 sec rest

Chest tall, slow nasal breathing, controlled steps.
